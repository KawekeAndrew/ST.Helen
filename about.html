Mission

M

Our mission at St. Helen Primary School is to provide a safe, nurturing, and inclusive learning environment that inspires our students to reach their full potential. We are committed to fostering academic excellence, promoting personal growth, and instilling a love of learning in our students. We strive to empower our students with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to become responsible, compassionate, and active members of their communities. We are dedicated to cultivating a strong sense of belonging, respect, and appreciation for diversity in all aspects of our school culture."

Vision

V

Our vision at St. Helen Primary School is to be a leading center of academic excellence and innovation, where every student is empowered to achieve their full potential. We strive to provide a dynamic and inclusive learning environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning. We are committed to building a strong community that values diversity, respect, and collaboration, and that actively engages in the education and growth of our students. Through our dedication to excellence and our unwavering commitment to our values, we aim to make a positive impact on the lives of our students, families, and communities
